Overview

This website offers analysis and graphical depictions of climate data from over 500 clities across the globe. The conditions measured are the temperature(F), humidity(%), wind speed(mph), and cloudiness(%) of each city plotted against its latitude.

The data was gathered by accessing the OpenWeather API and entering the results into a Pandas database. From there, the data was plotted using Matplotlib functionalities.
